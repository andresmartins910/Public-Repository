A timeline of Chagdud Tulku Rinpoche's life:

  • 1930 - Born in Kham province, eastern Tibet.

  • 1934 - Recognized as a Tulku, receives rigorous training and deepens his studies in intense retreats.

  • 1959 - Escape from China's communist invasion, living exiled in refugee camps in India and Nepal.

  • 1979 - Establishes in the United States of America.

  • 1983 - At the request of his western students, he established the Chagdud Gonpa Foundation, a successful network of centers of the Nyingma lineage of Vajrayana Buddhism.

  • 1994 - Moves to Brazil and establishes the Chagdud Gonpa Brasil, starting the construction of its main center, Khadro Ling, in the state of Rio Grande do Sul.

  • 2002 - Chagdud Tulku's Rinpoche Parinirvana.
